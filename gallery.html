Saif-ul-Mulook is a stunning alpine lake located in the Kaghan Valley of Pakistan's Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province. It's often considered one of the most beautiful and scenic lakes in Pakistan. Here are some key details and information about Saif-ul-Mulook

Loation

: Saif-ul-Mulook Lake is situated in the northern part of Pakistan, in the Kaghan Valley of the Himalayas. It's at an elevation of approximately 3,224 meters (10,578 feet) above sea level.

Scenic Beauty

The lake is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush green meadows, and thick forests, Saif-ul-Mulook offers a picturesque and serene landscape that attracts tourists, hikers, and nature enthusiasts from around the world.

Legend of Saif-ul-Mulook:/h2>

The lake is famous for the folklore associated with it. It is named after a Persian prince, Saif-ul-Mulook, who fell in love with a fairy named Princess Badri-ul-Jamal. The tale is often recited in the region and adds to the mystique of the lake

Lalazar is a beautiful and scenic plateau located near Naran in the Kaghan Valley of Pakistan's Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province. It is a popular tourist destination known for its breathtaking natural beauty, lush green meadows, and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Here's some more information about Lalazar:

Location

Lalazar is situated at an elevation of approximately 3,200 meters (10,499 feet) above sea level. It is about 21 kilometers (13 miles) from Naran and can be reached via a jeep track from Naran town

Viewpoints

Lalazar offers panoramic views of several prominent peaks in the region, including Malika Parbat, Falak Sar, and Makra Peak. The combination of the meadows, forests, and the backdrop of these majestic mountains makes it a paradise for nature photographers.

Location

Babu Sar Top is situated in the Babusar Pass area, which connects the Kaghan Valley with the Chilas Valley in Gilgit-Baltistan. It's part of the Babusar Pass, a high mountain pass in the Western Himalayas.

Scenic Beauty

Babu Sar Top is renowned for its stunning panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, including peaks like Laila Peak, Malika Parbat, and other prominent peaks in the region. The pass offers a breathtaking vantage point for taking in the natural beauty of the Himalayas.
